Privacy Policy: Verified GPS Camera
Last Updated: February 27, 2026

At Verified GPS Camera, operated by Aman Gupta, we prioritize user privacy and data transparency. This policy explains our data practices for the Verified GPS Camera application.

1. Information We Collect
Location Data: We access real-time GPS coordinates (Latitude, Longitude) only when the camera is active to overlay verification data onto your photos.

Camera Access: Required to capture photos within the App.

2. Data Access & Storage (Crucial)
No Access to Local Storage: We do not have access to your device's private gallery or any photos stored locally in the App’s internal directory.

Uploaded Images Only: Our servers and the Admin can only access, process, and store images that you explicitly choose to upload via the "Upload" action.

Private Offline Files: Any photo that remains in the "Files" tab without being uploaded is strictly private to your device and is never transmitted to our backend.

3. Identity & Account Data
We collect your email or phone number (e.g., owsmboy7383@gmail.com) via Google Sign-In or Firebase OTP to manage your account tiers and enforcement of upload limits (Guest, Standard, or Pro).

4. Data Retention (Nightly Cleanup)
Auto-Delete: To maintain server integrity, a nightly cleanup occurs at 2:00 AM. Uploaded photos are permanently deleted based on the set retention period (3, 6, or 12 months).

Sync Status: Uploaded photos will show a green cloud badge, while local-only photos will show a grey cloud, indicating they are not on our servers.

5. Security & Anti-Hack
We use UUID generation and AsyncStorage whitelist verification to ensure only authentic photos are processed. Unauthorized or "spoofed" files are blocked during the upload attempt.
