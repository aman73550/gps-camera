## 📱 Project Overview: GPS Camera without ADS

Ek high-security, professional GPS camera app jo Google ke Material 3 design par based hai. Iska main maqsad accurate geo-tagging aur secure server uploading hai.

### 🏗️ Core Architecture (The Two-Tab System)

App me sirf do main navigation buttons honge jo bottom bar me rahenge:

1. **Camera Tab:** Direct camera access, live geo-data, aur integrated gallery shortcut.
2. **Files Tab:** Internal gallery, Serial number search, aur QR scanner.

---

## 🛠️ Detailed Feature Specifications

### 1. Camera Engine & Image Processing

* **Live Preview:** Camera screen par real-time Latitude, Longitude aur Address display hoga.
* **Capture Logic:** Jab user photo click karega, app ye steps background me follow karega:
* **Serial Number:** Ek unique ID generate hogi (e.g., `IMG-20260226-001`).
* **QR Code:** Is serial number ka ek QR code banega.
* **Overlay (Burn-in):** Photo ke **Bottom-Left** me Geo-details (Lat/Long/Address) aur **Bottom-Right** me QR Code + Serial Number permanent overlap ho jayenge.


* **Compression:** Upload se pehle photo ko mobile-side par hi compress kiya jayega ($200KB$ se $300KB$ ke beech) bina quality kharab kiye.

### 2. Files & Gallery (The "No-Hack" Zone)

* **Strict Internal Storage:** App kisi bhi system gallery (Phone Gallery/Photos) se image nahi uthayega. Sirf wahi photos dikhayi dengi jo is app se click hui hain.
* **SQLite Whitelist:** Har click ki gayi photo ka record ek local database (SQLite) me save hoga. Upload button tabhi kaam karega jab photo ka record database me milega.
* **Search & Scan:**
* **Search:** Serial number type karke photo dhund sakte hain.
* **Scan:** Kisi dusri printed photo ke QR ko scan karke uski digital copy app me search kar sakte hain.



### 3. User Tiers & Upload Limits

* **Guest User:** Max 20 images upload karne ki permission.
* **Logged-in User:** Unlimited uploads (Firebase Authentication ke through).

---

## 🎨 Design Guidelines (Google Material 3)

App ka interface bilkul Google Photos ya Google Lens jaisa dikhna chahiye:

* **Typography:** Google Sans font ka use karein.
* **Colors:** Tonal palettes (Light/Soft Blue and White).
* **Shapes:** Button aur Cards ke corners **28dp** rounded hone chahiye.
* **Navigation:** Bottom Navigation Bar with M3 icons.

---

## 🔒 Security Protocol (Anti-Hack System)

Koi bhi user fake image upload na kar sake, iske liye ye 3 lock lagaye gaye hain:

1. **Hidden Folder:** Photos ko `getApplicationDocumentsDirectory()` me save kiya jayega (jo normal gallery me nahi dikhti).
2. **No Image Picker:** App me phone gallery se photo select karne ka koi option nahi hoga.
3. **Path Verification:** Upload ke waqt app check karega ki image file app ke private folder me hai ya nahi. Agar user manually file waha move karega, toh SQLite database me uska entry na hone ki wajah se upload fail ho jayega.
